# Másodfokú egyenlőtlenségek
#### a. feladat
- $x^{2} + 6x - 7 < 0$
- $x^{2} + 6x - 7 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = 6$
    - $c = -7$
- $\frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{6^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-7)}}{2} =$
- $= \frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{36 + 28}}{2} =$
- $= \frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{64}}{2} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{2}{2} = 1$
    - $x2 = - \frac{14}{2} = -7$
- Megoldás: $-7 < x < 1$
#### b. feladat
- $x^{2} -2x - 8 \ge 0$
- $x^{2} -2x -8 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = -2$
    - $c = -8$
- $\frac{2 \pm \sqrt{(-2)^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot (-8)}}{2} =$
- $= \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{4 + 32}}{2} =$
- $= \frac{2 \pm \sqrt{36}}{2} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{8}{2} = 4$
    - $x2 = \frac{-4}{2} = -2$
- Megoldás:
    - $x \ge 4$
    - $x \le -2$
#### c. feladat
- $x^{2} - 9x + 18 < 0$
- $x^{2} - 9x + 18 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = -9$
    - $c = 18$
- $\frac{9 \pm \sqrt{(-9)^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 18}}{2 \cdot 1} = \frac{9 \pm \sqrt{81 - 72}}{2} = \frac{9 \pm \sqrt{9}}{2} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{12}{2} = 6$
    - $x2 = \frac{6}{2} = 3$
- Megoldás: $3 < x < 6$
#### d. feladat
- $x^{2} - 8x + 19 \le 0$
- $x^{2} - 8x + 19 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = -8$
    - $c = 19$
- $\frac{8 \pm \sqrt{(-8)^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 19}}{2 \cdot 1} = \frac{8 \pm \sqrt{64 - 76}}{2} = \frac{8 \pm \sqrt{-12}}{2}$
- Nincs valós megoldás
#### e. feladat
- $x^{2} + 7x + 6 > 0$
- $x^{2} + 7x + 6 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = 7$
    - $c = 6$
- $\frac{-7 \pm \sqrt{7^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 6}}{2 \cdot 1} = \frac{-7 \pm \sqrt{49 - 24}}{2} = \frac{-7 \pm \sqrt{25}}{2} =$
    - $x1 = - \frac{2}{2} = -1$
    - $x2 = - \frac{12}{2} = -6$
- Megoldás: $x < -6$ és $x > -1$
#### f. feladat
- $x^{2} + 6x + 10 > 0$
- $x^{2} + 6x + 10 = 0$
    - $a = 1$
    - $b = 6$
    - $c = 10$
- $\frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{6^{2} - 4 \cdot 1 \cdot 10}}{2 \cdot 1} = \frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{36 - 40}}{2} = \frac{-6 \pm \sqrt{-4}}{2}$
- $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}$ megoldás
#### g. feladat
- $2x^{2} - x - 6 \le 0$
- $2x^{2} - x - 6 = 0$
    - $a = 2$
    - $b = -1$
    - $c = -6$
- $\frac{1 \pm \sqrt{(-1)^{2} - 4 \cdot 2 \cdot (-6)}}{2 \cdot 2} = \frac{1 \pm \sqrt{49}}{4} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{8}{4} = 2$
    - $x2 = \frac{-6}{4} = \frac{-3}{2} = -1.5$
- Megoldás: $\frac{-3}{2} \le x \le 2$
#### h. feladat
- $4x^{2} + 12x + 5 < 0$
- $4x^{2} + 12x + 5 = 0$
    - $a = 4$
    - $b = 12$
    - $c = 5$
- $\frac{-12 \pm \sqrt{12^{2} - 4 \cdot 4 \cdot 5}}{2 \cdot 4} = \frac{-12 \pm \sqrt{144 - 80}}{8} = \frac{-12 \pm \sqrt{64}}{8} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{-4}{8} = \frac{-1}{2} = -0.5$
    - $x2 = \frac{-20}{8} = \frac{-5}{2} = -2.5$
- Megoldás: $-2.5 < x < -0.5$
#### i. feladat
- $3x^{2} - 8x + 7 \ge 0$
- $3x^{2} - 8x + 7 = 0$
    - $a = 3$
    - $b = -8$
    - $c = 7$
- $\frac{8 \pm \sqrt{(-8)^{2} - 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 7}}{2 \cdot 3} = \frac{8 \pm \sqrt{64 - 84}}{6} = \frac{8 \pm \sqrt{-20}}{6}$
- $\forall x \in \mathbb{R}$ megoldás
#### j. feladat
- $15x^{2} + 11x + 2 > 0$
- $15x^{2} + 11x + 2 = 0$
    - $a = 15$
    - $b = 11$
    - $c = 2$
- $\frac{-11 \pm \sqrt{11^{2} - 4 \cdot 15 \cdot 2}}{2 \cdot 15} = \frac{-11 \pm \sqrt{121 - 120}}{30} = \frac{-11 \pm \sqrt{1}}{30} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{-10}{30} = \frac{-1}{3} = -0.3$
    - $x2 = \frac{-12}{30} = \frac{-2}{5} = -0.4$
- Megoldás: $x < -\frac{2}{5}$ vagy $x > -\frac{1}{3}$
#### k. feladat
- $35x^{2} - 46x + 15 \le 0$
- $35x^{2} - 46x + 15 = 0$
    - $a = 35$
    - $b = -46$
    - $c = 15$
- $\frac{46 \pm \sqrt{(-46)^{2} - 4 \cdot 35 \cdot 15}}{2 \cdot 35} = \frac{46 \pm \sqrt{2116 - 2100}}{70} = \frac{46 \pm \sqrt{16}}{70} =$
    - $x1 = \frac{50}{70} = \frac{5}{7} = 0.7142857143$
    - $x2 = \frac{42}{70} = \frac{3}{5} = 0.6$
- Megoldás: $0.6 \le x \le 0.714...$
#### l. feladat
- $2x^{2} + 3x + 3 < 0$
- $2x^{2} + 3x + 3 = 0$
    - $a = 2$
    - $b = 3$
    - $c = 3$
- $\frac{-3 \pm \sqrt{3^{2} - 4 \cdot 2 \cdot 3}}{2 \cdot 2} = \frac{-3 \pm \sqrt{9 - 24}}{4} = \frac{-3 \pm \sqrt{-15}}{4}$
- Nincs valós megoldás
